Description

Dear exoplanet builder, Your mission is to build a planet for all of us to live in. You will have to be wise and careful when selecting parameters for a star and a planet you are about to compose. Your goal is to survive and understand how to create a world hospitable to life. Maybe it will become our new holiday destination?! However, there are over 100 Billion planets in our Galaxy called Milky-Way, 4 000 were confirmed as exoplanets but only nearly 50 are in the habitable zone. Embrace yourself and take a selfie to see what you would look like in your newly built exoplanet!
