Description

Your mission is to build a planet for all of us to live in. You will have to be wise and careful when selecting parameters for a star and a planet you are about to compose. Your goal is to survive and understand how to create the world hospitable to life. Maybe it will become our new holiday destination?! However, there are over 100 Billion planets in our Galaxy called Milky-Way, 4 000 were confirmed as exoplanets but only nearly 50 are in the habitable zone. Embrace yourself and take a selfie to see how will you look in your newly built exoplanet!
